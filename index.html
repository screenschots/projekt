The BRAT cave

Legend has it that the BRAT Cave was founded by Madam Roxie, a former fashion mogul and underground icon who vanished from the public eye after a dramatic fallout with the elites of high society. Tired of playing by the rules, she disappeared—only to return stronger, building a hidden empire of fashion, wealth, and rebellion beneath the city. Inside the BRAT Cave: Entering the BRAT Cave is like stepping into a pink-tinted dream world. Chandeliers made of diamond-studded lip gloss tubes hang from the ceiling. Neon signs glow with phrases like “Stay Cute, Stay Dangerous” and “Lip Gloss Before War.” The air smells like vanilla, cash, and a hint of rebellion.
